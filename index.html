About Me

A self-taught Data Analyst

A graduate with a degree in Computer Technology, I developed a strong interest in data analytics. After working in the retail and banking sectors, I became fascinated by the technology behind what I observed. I seized this opportunity to teach myself the skills needed to become an analyst, building foundations with self-learning in Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau. I am now eager to advance my skills and career further, having experience working with Excel and Power BI.

I am a detail-oriented and analytical thinker with the skills and motivation to be a valuable asset to any team. Excited to bring my enthusiasm and drive to a company where I can make a meaningful contribution.
